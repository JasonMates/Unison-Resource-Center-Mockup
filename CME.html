About Us

Welcome to Unison CME, a privately held medical education organization in the process of seeking CME accreditation.

Unison is dedicated to supporting healthcare organizations and providers through the development of high-quality, independent, and evidence-based educational activities.

Our educational programs are designed to align with the standards and requirements of the Accreditation Council for Continuing Medical Education (ACCME), with a focus on independence, balance, and scientific rigor.

Leveraging AI-integrated technologies alongside sound educational design, we identify practice gaps, personalize learning experiences, and measure outcomes across knowledge, competence, and performance.

Collaboration with industry-leading professionals and strategic service offerings, Unison supports innovative CME program development that promotes continuous professional development and improved patient care.

Services offered with Unison CME:

Accreditation

  • CME Symposium/ Conference Accreditation
  • Reoccurring Schedule Series (RSS) Accreditation
  • Enduring Materials accreditation

CME Administration

  • Symposium/ Conference registration
  • CME Certificate management
  • CME Transcripts
  • MOC program reporting / management
Health Organization CME packages/ Service bundles available upon request

Unison Research & Education is actively pursuing CME accreditation. CME credit will be offered in accordance with accreditation requirements upon approval.
